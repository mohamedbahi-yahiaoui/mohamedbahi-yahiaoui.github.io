I am **Mohamed Bahi Yahiaoui**, a research engineer in deep learning working at the intersection of **scientific machine learning**, **reliable AI**, and **physics-based modeling**.

My research focuses on **uncertainty quantification**, **out-of-distribution detection**, **neural surrogate modeling**, and **dependable machine learning for high-stakes scientific applications**. I am also interested in **computer vision**, **representation learning**, and **LLM applications for science**.
I completed my PhD between **Université Grenoble Alpes** and **CEA Cadarache**, under the supervision of [Julyan Arbel](https://www.julyanarbel.com/) ([Inria Statify](https://team.inria.fr/statify/)) and [Geoffrey Daniel](https://uranie.cea.fr/presentation/team/geoffreydaniel/language/en) ([CEA Saclay](https://uranie.cea.fr/presentation/team/index/language/en)).
