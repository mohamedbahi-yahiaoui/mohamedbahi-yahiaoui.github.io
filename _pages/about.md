      runtime monitoring, uncertainty-aware modeling, and neural surrogates for expensive simulations.

      <strong>OpenCalphad</strong> and <strong>CARACAS/ALCYONE</strong>, where the central question is how to make learned models fast,
      stable, and usable in real simulation workflows.
